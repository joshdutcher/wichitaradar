Streaming Video from Storm Chasers

  • Ryan Hall More an online weather-caster than a storm chaser, but he includes some storm-chasers on his stream. Also runs a nonprofit that helps fill in gaps for storm victims that aren't served by Salvation Army and FEMA.

Live Audio/Radio

  • Broadcastify Excellent collection of scanner feeds from Sedgwick County & Wichita area LEO agencies, emergency services, etc
  • KFDI radio during storms, hands down the absolute best local radio storm coverage, with mobile spotters. Country music the rest of the time.

Other Local Stuff

  • Evergy Power Outages Evergy's map of current power outages
  • KanDrive Camera Tours Multiple webcam views of traffic and road conditions along a Wichita area route of your choice, on a single web page. Choose from US-54, I-235, I-35, K-96, and I-135

Interesting Reading

  • The T-Word You Couldn't Talk About It used to be against NOAA's rules to mention the word tornado in a weather forecast.
